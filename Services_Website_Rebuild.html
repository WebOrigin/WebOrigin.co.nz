  Website Rebuild
  
 

WebOrigin commits to develop professional websites to meet your needs. Many companies built relevantly simple websites when they initially set up their businesses. While their businesses grow, their existing websites may not be sufficient to function in a satisfied level. Do you want to make improvement of web graphic design? Do you want to add more function to your website? Or do you want to attract more traffic to your website? Whatever the reason that you want to rebuild your website, we have the ideas here at WebOrigin to help you achieve the new goals of your business. Talk to us today and let us to find out the solution for you.
 
  1. Does your website require better aesthetic work? (e.g. inconsistent colour scheme to your brand)
  2. Does your website need new pages to display new products and services?
  3. Does your website require restructuring? (e.g. illogical layout of pages)
  4. Do some pages take a long time to load?
  5. Does your site contain outdated content? (e.g. old version of corporate logo)
  6. Are there inconsistencies on your web pages? (e.g. inconsistent font)
  7. Does your website have poor quality graphics? (e.g. jagged edges, long download times)
  8. Do your competitor's websites look more professional than yours?
  9. Do you want to upgrade some functions on your website? (e.g. customer enquiry system)
  10. Are there any dead links?
  11. Does your website appear unsatisfactorily in certain web browsers?
  12. Do you lack the technical skills to update your website?
